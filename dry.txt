Don't Repeat Yourself (DRY) Principle
    
    Every peace of knowledge(data) must have a single , representation within a system.

    "Key Concepts"
     -Knowledge,Not Just Code
     -Single Source of Truth
     -WET(The Opposite): Systems that do not follow DRY are often are called WET("Write everything twice","Waste Everyone's Time", or "We enjoy typing")

    Benefits of DRY
        -Maintainability
        -Readability
        -Consistency
        -Reduced Development Time
